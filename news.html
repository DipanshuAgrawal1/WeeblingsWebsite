'Renai Flops' Unveils Production Staff, Additional Cast, First Promo, Fall 2022 Premiere


The official website of the Renai Flops (Love Flops) original television anime revealed on Tuesday one additional cast, the production staff, the opening theme, and a promotional video. Manga 'Dark Gathering' Gets TV Anime in 2023


Publisher Shueisha opened an official website for the television anime adaptation of Kenichi Kondou's Dark Gathering manga on Tuesday, revealing the main staff, a teaser visual (pictured), and a teaser promotional video. The anime is set to premiere in 2023. Hiroshi Ikehata (Tonikaku Kawaii) is directing the anime at OLM. Kondou started serializing the horror manga in March 2019 in Jump SQ. magazine. The ninth volume was released on March 4, with the tenth volume to be shipped on August 4.

'Migi to Dali' Reveals Main Staff Members


Production company Kadokawa opened an official website for the anime adaptation of Nami Sano's Migi to Dali (Migi & Dali) manga on Monday, revealing the main staff and a teaser visual (pictured). Staff Director, Series Composition: Mankyuu (30-sai no Hoken Taiiku, Cinderella Girls Gekijou) Character Design: Ayumi Nishihata (Urasekai Picnic, Kuromukuro chief animation director) Music: Hiroko Sebu (Kimi no Suizou wo Tabetai) Animation Production Cooperation: CompTown Studio: Frontier Engine, GEEK TOYS The comedy manga was serialized in Harta magazine from July 2017 to November 2021. Kadokawa shipped the seventh and final volume on December 15.
